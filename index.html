My darling,


Three months ago, we were just two best friends sharing secrets, laughing at things no one else understood, living in a world that felt entirely our own. It was simple then, the kind of connection people spend a lifetime searching for.


I still think about those little moments… like when our hands brushed and we both pretended it meant nothing. Just an accident. Just one of those things. But deep down, something was already beginning, quietly, slowly changing everything.


Somewhere between the late-night conversations and the way you would say, “you are the only one who gets me,” my heart shifted. Without even realizing it, you became more than my best friend. You became someone I could not ignore, someone I did not want to imagine my life without.


Now here we are, three months in, and it still feels a little unreal sometimes. Like we are standing in something beautiful, something a little scary, but so worth it. I would not lie, I was afraid at first. Afraid of risking what we had. But I think what scared me even more was the idea of never knowing what we could become.


And now I know.


You have shown me a love that feels natural, steady, and real. The kind that does not erase what we were, but builds on it into something even stronger.


You inspire me in ways I cannot always explain. Your strength, your light, the way you carry yourself through everything. It is something I admire deeply. I have always loved art, the way it tells stories and makes people feel something real… but you, you are something else entirely. The most beautiful, effortless masterpiece I have ever known.


And somehow, I am the one who gets to love you.


These past three months have meant more to me than I can fully put into words. And if this is just the beginning, then I cannot wait to see where this story takes us.

Forever yours, ♥
